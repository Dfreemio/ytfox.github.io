INFORMATION YTfox COLLECTS

We want you to understand the types of information we collect as you use our services.

We collect information to provide better services to all our users — from figuring out basic stuff like which language you speak, to more complex things like which ads you’ll find most useful.

When you’re not signed in to YTfox using your Youtube Account, we store the information we collect with unique identifiers tied to the application, or device you’re using. This helps us do things like maintain your language preferences across sessions.

When you’re signed in YTfox using Youtube account, we also collect certain information attached to your Youtube account. We collect your email address, your name, and your profile picture, in addition to basic information like location and language collected irrespective of signing in.

All the personal sensitive information like email address are stored in their encrypted/hashed forms on our servers.

PERMISSIONS REQUESTED BY YTfox ANDROID APPLICATION AND WHY ARE THEY REQUIRED 

INTERNET
Internet connection is required by the app for fetching all the feeds and streaming music. In addition the app talks to our servers over ssl connections via active internet connection.
ACCESS NETWORK STATE
This permission is required to determine the type and quality of internet connection. This helps customize and improve user experience and avoid long loading times.
READ PHONE STATE
YTfox need to know when you're in call, it will pause and resume music automatically.
WAKE LOCK
In order to play music in background, or execute long running task like Download, YTfox need to keep CPU awake until the task is done, or music is paused.
STORAGE ACCESS
Storage access is required to write all the files you download to your phone storage. We do not read or analyze any file on your device, including the ones you download via YTfox.
COARSE LOCATION
We need this permission to collect user’s approximate location so that we can show personalized and relevant suggestions.
REQUEST INSTALL PACKAGES
YTfox need this permision to update new version by itself, you're able to confirm before the installation begin.
